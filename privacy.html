Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Anqor Studios ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

This policy applies to our website at anqorstudios.com and to information we collect in the course of providing our services. It does not apply to the systems we build on behalf of clients those are governed by their own data policies and agreements.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you provide directly:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and company name when you fill in a contact form, lead form, or book a strategy call.
  • Messages and answers you submit through our website or Meta lead forms.
  • Communications you send us via email or WhatsApp.

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic analytics data such as pages visited, time on site, and referral source. We use this to understand how people find and use our website.
  • IP address and browser type, collected automatically by our hosting provider.

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes on our own website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and qualify potential engagements.
  • Communicate with you about your project, including via WhatsApp and email.
  • Send you relevant information about our services where you have indicated interest.
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors engage with it.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data to train AI models.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interests to respond to enquiries and conduct our business.
  • Contractual necessity to deliver services you have engaged us for.
  • Consent where you have explicitly opted in to receive communications from us.
  • Legal obligation where required by applicable law.

4. How We Share Your Information

We only share your data with third parties where necessary to operate our business:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers our website is hosted on Vercel. Their privacy policy applies to data processed through their platform.
  • Communication tools we use WhatsApp (Meta) and email to communicate with clients and prospects.
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) if you interact with our ads or submit a lead form through Meta's platform, Meta's data policy applies to that interaction. We receive the information you submit and process it under this policy.
  • AI API providers where we use third-party AI APIs (e.g. Anthropic, OpenAI) in the course of delivering services, those providers operate under commercial Data Processing Agreements that prohibit training on client data.

We do not share your data with any other third parties without your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Enquiry and lead data is retained for up to 24 months from last contact.
  • Client project data is retained for up to 5 years for legal and accounting purposes.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. These include encrypted communications, access controls, and working only with reputable third-party providers.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to maintaining appropriate safeguards.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Deletion request that we delete your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Restriction request that we limit how we process your data.
  • Portability request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@anqorstudios.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. International Transfers

Anqor Studios is based in Dubai, UAE. If you are located in the EEA, UK, or another jurisdiction with data transfer restrictions, your data may be transferred to and processed in the UAE or other countries where our service providers operate.

Where required, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including standard contractual clauses or reliance on providers with adequate data protection frameworks.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify active clients by email. Continued use of our website or services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
