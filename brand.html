07 - Design Principles

PRINCIPLES

Six rules that govern every design decision across Anqor Studios products. When in doubt, come back here.

01
Confidence, not noise
Every element earns its place. No decorative flourishes. No gradient fills. No glow effects. The palette does the work - restraint signals premium. If you're adding something to make it "look better", remove it.
02
Accent is a signal, not a decoration
Green accent and Gold appear only where action or importance is required - CTAs, selected states, labels, key data. Overusing accent colour kills its impact. When in doubt, leave it out.
03
Depth through layering
Background → Surface → Card creates a depth hierarchy. Every card should sit one stop above its parent. Never skip levels or reverse the order. Depth communicates importance - use it deliberately.
04
Typography carries tone
Bebas Neue (display) brings authority and scale. Inter (UI) brings clarity and speed. Playfair (product serif) brings warmth and intelligence. Never mix the roles. Step labels are always accent colour, always uppercase.
05
Space is structure
Generous padding inside cards. Clear separation between sections. White space is not emptiness - it is the structure that makes content readable and commands premium positioning. Tight = cheap.
06
Consistency across every touchpoint
The website, XANA dashboard, SIGNAL Hub, Client Portal, decks, and social all speak the same visual language. If a new component needs to be built, reference this guide first and document the new pattern here.
